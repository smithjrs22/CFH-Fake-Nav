Step by Step Directions to Make Shrimp and Grits

  1. Boil two cups of water.
  2. Once the water reaches a boil. Add salt or cajun seasoning. Add one spoonful of butter. And 6 tablespoons of grits.
  3. Lower the temperature on the stove top to low. Cover and let it summer for 5 minutes occassionally stirring.
  4. Add the shrimp to the a pan with one tablespoon of oil or water. Season with the seasoning of choice. The shrimp is ready when it is pink and is the shape of a "c."
  5. In five minutes once the grits are thick to your liking remove from the stove top.
  6. Add the shrimp to the grits. Add cheese to the grits to make cheesy shrimp and grits.
